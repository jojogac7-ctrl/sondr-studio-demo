Who We Are

Athletic Pilates.
Retreat Energy.

Sondr Studio is Nairobi's premier athletic reformer pilates studio. We combine the precision and challenge of reformer pilates with a calm, considered environment that makes every class feel like a moment of recovery.

Whether you're new to Pilates or a seasoned practitioner, our small-group classes are intentionally programmed — to build strength, improve posture, enhance movement, and reconnect you with your body.

Founded by Gagan Ubi, Sondr was built around one belief: that your workout should feel as good as it makes you look.

New to Pilates?

Start with Foundation Four

Our beginner programme designed to build your confidence on the reformer across 4 small-group sessions. Learn essential movement patterns, build strength at your own pace, and feel ready for anything.

1

Reformer Basics

Meet the machine. Learn the springs, straps and how to move safely with control.

2

Core Connection

Understand your breath, your core and how they power every movement you make.

3

Full Body Flow

Put it all together. A full-body session building on what you've learned with growing confidence.

4

Ready to Move

You're now ready for any Sondr class. Your foundation is set. Let's go deeper.
